# Language Source and Rights Notice

This repository includes community language packs and experimental spelling
dictionaries for TeXisStudio. The files under `dictionaries/nah`,
`dictionaries/yua`, `dictionaries/tzh`, `dictionaries/mix`, and
`dictionaries/zap` are seed Hunspell wordlists intended to test spelling
support and invite review.

These seed dictionaries are not normative dictionaries. They contain extracted
candidate headwords only: no full source texts, no definitions, no examples,
and no substantial dictionary entries are redistributed here. They still need
native-speaker review, orthographic review, and source-rights review before a
stable release.

If you are a rights holder, author, editor, publisher, community authority, or
speaker who wants a correction, attribution update, removal, or licensing note
change, please open an issue in this repository.

## Attribution and Stability Policy

- Stable dictionaries must come from redistributable sources with clear license
  or permission records.
- Experimental seed dictionaries may remain in this repository only when their
  metadata and documentation mark them as non-stable and review-pending.
- A language pack must not claim stable spelling support until the relevant
  dictionary source, orthography, and redistribution rights have been reviewed.
- Community corrections are welcome, especially from speakers, teachers,
  linguists, and maintainers of the original lexical resources.

## Aviso en Español

Este repositorio incluye paquetes comunitarios de idioma y diccionarios
experimentales para TeXisStudio. Los archivos en `dictionaries/nah`,
`dictionaries/yua`, `dictionaries/tzh`, `dictionaries/mix` y `dictionaries/zap`
son listas semilla para Hunspell: sirven para probar el corrector ortográfico y
abrir revisión comunitaria.

Estos diccionarios semilla no son diccionarios normativos. Contienen candidatos
de palabras extraídas, pero no redistribuyen textos completos, definiciones,
ejemplos ni entradas sustanciales de las obras fuente. Antes de publicarse como
estables requieren revisión de hablantes, revisión ortográfica y revisión de
derechos de las fuentes.

Si eres titular de derechos, autor, editor, institución, autoridad comunitaria o
hablante y quieres corregir atribuciones, pedir retiro, ajustar una nota de
licencia o proponer mejoras, abre un issue en este repositorio.
